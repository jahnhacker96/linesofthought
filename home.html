Welcome to My Gallery!

I'm Jeanette Ahn, and this is a personal archive of art, architecture, and sculpture that has moved me—across time, style, and place.

Each piece you’ll find here is more than a masterpiece; it’s a moment I’ve chosen to hold onto. I’ve included my reflections alongside each work to share how it resonates with me and how it fits into the broader story of art history—how form, emotion, and time meet.

This is not just a gallery. It’s a conversation across centuries.
